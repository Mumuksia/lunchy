Tacos & Tequila is a Mexican-American restaurant located at Tredje Långgatan in Linnéstaden, Gothenburg. The restaurant was opened in the fall of 2014 and has a menu serving classic Mexican-American dishes.
Open every day. Lunch is served every Monday – Friday 11.30 – 14.00.
